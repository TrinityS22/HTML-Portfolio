My top 3 movies of all time

Studio Ghibli Edition

My Neighbor Totoro

This movie has beautiful images of the scenery and the nastalgia.
This movie is also very heartwarming and a great family movie.

Spirited Away

I would rank this as my second favorite movie. Everyone knew this one was
definitley going to be up here. It is a very popular movie and it is amazing
it gives me a very summer, peaceful vibe, and many lessons to be learned.

How's Moving Castle

Lastly I would say this movie. It is a good watch again with the family. I would say
it does not have that same summer and peaceful feeling that you would
get with the previous two movies. This movie is more of a thriller to watch. More
action is added into this movie. Still child friendly and funny to watch.
